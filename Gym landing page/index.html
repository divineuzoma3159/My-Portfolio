Beginner Guidance

What You
Need to Know

01

Form First, Always

Real progress comes from perfect form. Build a solid foundation before adding weight. Stay in a calorie deficit and take proper rest between sets — these are the three pillars of beginner gains.

02

Structured Plan

Monday — Back, Lats & Arms
Tuesday — Shoulders & Chest
Wednesday — Legs & Abs
Friday — Back, Lats & Arms
Saturday — Shoulders, Chest & Legs

03

Supportive Environment

Most beginners worry about being judged at the gym. The reality? People are there to help and encourage you. Leave the anxiety at the door — you belong there from day one.

04

Track Everything

Log your workouts and meals. Knowing your calorie intake keeps you on track for a deficit. What gets measured gets improved — and consistency compounds over time.
