Tips to guide you

Beginner-friendly guidance

When working out make sure your form is good because progress mainly comes when your form is good and also when you are in a calorie-deficit and also make sure to take rests in between sets also do cardio after every strength training that's more advisable

Structured work-out plan

• Monday - Back + Lats + Arms

• Tuesday - Shoulders + Chest

• Wednesday - Legs + Abs

• Friday - Back and Lats + Arms

• Saturday - Shoulders + Chest

Supportive Environment

Some people don't like going to the gym because of thier anxieties but the people of the gym do not judge you no matter what size you are instead they encourage and support you some even help when you're having problems like fixing your form so there's no need to be worried about what people would think of you in the gym

Track your progress

It is advisable to track your workout and what you eat so that you'll know if you are making progress or not. This is highly advisable
